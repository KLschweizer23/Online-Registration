Banner Making

How to compete:

Create a banner that shows the church name. The banner should signify or symbolize your church name


Requirements:

- 1 meter x 1 meter size cloth (tela) in any kind and color

- Only use any recyclable material or any scraps

- Church name must be visible (e.g. Living Temple, Living Word, Tabernacle of Faith, etc.)


Penalty

- Not following the given requirements could result to a deduction of points


Time for presentation:

- First Night (Entrance of colors)

Tambourine Dance Competition

How to compete:

Perform a dance composed of tambourine players only


Requirements:

- 1 Christian song as background music

- No Member Limit


Penalty

- Not following the given requirements could result to a deduction of points.


Time for presentation:

- First Night

Song Composition Competition

How to compete:

Make a song that is relevant to the theme "Be Transformed" with an acoustic instrument.


Requirements:

- Maximum of 3 members, 1 for acoustic musician and 1 or 2 for singer/s

- Length must be within 2 - 3 minutes.


Penalty

- Not following the given requirements could result to a deduction of points.


Time for presentation:

- Last Night

Extemporaneous Speaking

Mechanics:

Compose of 3 members, one person for English, one person for Filipino, and one for Bisaya.

The first person (English) will choose the topic then will be given enough time for preperation to share his/her thoughts in English Language.

Next person will then translate to Filipino, then to Bisaya depending on the team's strategy to either pause every sentence or phrase, but not every word.


Requirements:

- Male or Female

- Member of any Apple Division Foursquare Gospel Church and non-sympathizer


Penalty

- Not following the given requirements are not allowed to participate

- Offensive words and actions will be remove immediately in game or not, Sportmanship is encouraged


Time for the Game:

- Last Night
