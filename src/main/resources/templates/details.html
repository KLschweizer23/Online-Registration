Apple Division Mini Camp


We, the United Foursquare Youth of Apple Division in North Central Mindanao District is holding our Mini-Camp this coming July 06-08, 2022 (Wednesday - Friday) with a theme "Be Transformed". There will be 2 nights and 2 days of fellowship, fun, and praises to our Lord Jesus Christ!

Venue


The Pastors of Apple Division has selected Living Word Foursquare Gospel Church - New Bataan, Davao de Oro as the venue for the up-coming mini camp.

Registration Information


There will be a total of Php 300.00 for registration and meals, all meals are provided by the division. This website is for pre-registration purposes only, please be guided by your local presidents and all transactions must be made personal. You can register on this website and inform your local presidents for the approval of your registration.
